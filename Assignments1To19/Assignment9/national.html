Floods in Assam

The recent floods in Assam have caused widespread devastation,
affecting more than five lakh people across over twenty districts.
Heavy pre-monsoon rains, coupled with the sudden release of water from dams,
led to rivers overflowing and inundating large areas of the Brahmaputra valley.
Many villages and towns, including parts of Guwahati, were submerged, forcing thousands to take shelter in relief camps.
