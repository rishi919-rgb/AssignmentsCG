We're enabling the next
generation of computing.

AI is touching our lives and driving breakthroughs in healthcare, finance, autonomous systems, and countless other domains. Its ability to process vast amounts of data, derive insights, and automate complex tasks is unlocking new levels of efficiency and innovation. However, the exponential growth of AI workloads demands ever-greater computational power and more efficient data movement. Traditional electronic interconnects are struggling to keep up, necessitating a paradigm shift in AI infrastructure.

The answer is like night and day.

Celestial AI’s Photonic Fabric is a revolutionary technology that allows AI compute to be scaled—within package and package-to-package across multiple racks. It serves as the foundation for an optical scale-up network for accelerated computing, leaving behind the physical limitations of electronic interconnects. Offering terabytes of low-latency scale-up bandwidth, in-network computing, and in-network high-performance high-capacity memory, Photonic Fabric heralds a new dawn in AI infrastructure.

Switching to optical interconnects enables large multi-die packages and bigger scale-up domains, creating a leap into the next decade. Photonic Fabric-based products revolutionize AI data centers by facilitating large-scale-up domains of thousands of XPUs across multiple racks. This innovation enables continued scaling of model sizes, better reasoning and more efficient inference with increasing context lengths and batch sizes. Reducing the total cost of ownership unleashes new use cases and more profitable GenAI business models.

With an aggressive roadmap, a robust ecosystem of partners, and a tier-one supply chain, Celestial AI is not just reimagining AI data centers but delivering on the promise of an AI-driven, brighter, and more sustainable tomorrow. Because at its core, AI isn’t just about algorithms or hardware—it’s about people. It’s about unlocking creativity, solving humanity’s biggest challenges, and making the impossible possible. The future of AI is here, and with the Photonic Fabric, its potential is limitless.
