Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy curry made
with paneer, butter, and tomato gravy.

    Ingrediants:
  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Spices:
    • Garam masala
    • Chilli powder
    • Coriander powder
  • Cream
    Cooking Steps:
  1. Fry the paneer lightly.
  2. Prepare the tomato gravy.
  3. Add butter and spices.
  4. Mix in paneer cubes.
  5. Garnish with cream and coriander.
Cooking Terms:
Simmer
Cooking slowly at low temperature.
Puree
Smooth paste made from blending ingredients.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a sweet dessert made with
milk solids, fried, and soaked in sugar syrup.

    Ingrediants:
  • Khoya (milk solids)
  • Maida (Flour)
  • Sugar syrup
    • Sugar
    • Water
    • Cardamon
    • Rose water


    Cooking Steps:
  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Roll into small balls
  3. Deep fry until golden brown
  4. Dip in sugar syrup
  5. Serve warm
Cooking Terms:
Knead
Working the dough to develop structure.
Syrup
A thick sweet liquid made from sugar and water.
